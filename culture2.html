Akan Dressing

Akan people wore outfits of many different styles and materials.

Akan Art

Akan art is an art form that originated among the Akan people of West Africa.

Akan Religion

Akan religion remains practiced by some and is often syncretized with Christianity.

Akan Names

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born.

Male names

Kwasi/Kwesi (Sunday)
Kwadwo (Monday)
Kwabena(Tuesday)
Kwaku(Wednesday)
Kwao/Yao(Thursday)
Kwafi/Kofi(Friday)
Kwame/Kwamena(Saturday)

Female Names

Akosua/Esi(Sunday)
Adwoa(Monday)
Abena(Tuesday)
Akua(Wednesday)
Yaa(Thursday)
Afia/Afua(Friday)
Amma(Saturday)
